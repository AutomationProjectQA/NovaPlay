# NovaPlay — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 29 June 2026
**Contact:** support@novaplay.app

This policy explains what NovaPlay ("we", "the app") collects and how it is used.
It is written to be hosted publicly and linked from both app stores and from the
app's Settings ▸ Privacy · Terms. It mirrors the technical declarations in
[`docs/PRIVACY.md`](../PRIVACY.md), the App Store privacy label, and the Google
Play Data Safety form.

## What we collect

NovaPlay has **no account or login**. Your game progress, settings, and in-game
currency are stored **only on your device** and are not transmitted to us.

We (or our processors) may collect, in an **anonymous, non-identifying** form:

- **Crash and diagnostic logs** — to find and fix bugs (app functionality).
- **Gameplay analytics events** — e.g. level started/completed — to understand
  how the game is played and improve it (analytics).
- **Advertising data** — to display ads. Ads are **non-personalized by default**.

We do **not** collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, precise location,
photos, or device identifiers used to track you across other apps.

## How it's used

Solely to operate, fix, and improve NovaPlay, and to show ads that keep the game
free. We do not sell your personal data.

## Third-party services

When enabled, NovaPlay uses Google AdMob (ads), Google Analytics for Firebase
(analytics), and Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting). Their handling of data
is governed by Google's privacy policy. We configure them for non-personalized,
non-tracking use by default.

## Children

NovaPlay is suitable for all ages and is not directed at collecting personal
information from children. We only request anonymous analytics and serve
non-personalized ads.

## Your choices & rights

- **Delete your data:** uninstalling the app removes all on-device data. For any
  server-side analytics deletion, email support@novaplay.app.
- **Ads:** you can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
- Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR/CCPA), you may have rights to access or
  delete data held about you — contact us to exercise them.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the "Effective date" above will change and material
updates will be noted in the app's release notes.

## Contact

Questions? **support@novaplay.app**
